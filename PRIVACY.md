# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 9, 2026**

ListeningProject ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Audio Data**: We process audio data in real-time to provide translation and transcription services. This data is processed via Google Gemini API.
- **Authentication**: We use Supabase for user authentication and account management.
- **Local Storage**: We store session history and settings locally on your device, optionally encrypted with your personal Vault Key.
- **Subscription Data**: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and payment status.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve our translation and transcription services.
- To manage your account and subscriptions.
- To ensure the security of your data through encryption.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share only necessary data with our service providers (Google, Supabase, RevenueCat) to facilitate app functionality.

## 4. Security
Your data is encrypted locally using AES-GCM when using the Privacy Vault. We do not have access to your Vault Key.

## 5. Contact
For questions about this policy, please contact support@listeningproject.ai.
